About The System

Smart Medical Diagnosis System (SMDSS) is an AI-powered platform designed to help users identify possible diseases based on symptoms and provide guidance, precautions, and nearby hospital recommendations.

Try the System

🔬 AI Diagnosis

The system analyzes user symptoms using intelligent algorithms to predict possible diseases with high accuracy.

🏥 Hospital Finder

It locate the nearest hospital based on your real-time location and provide instant navigation routes.

💊 Treatment Guidance

Get recommended precautions and care instructions for each predicted condition.

🔒 Privacy First

Your health data is not stored permanently and is handled securely during your session.
